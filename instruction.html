You will see a solution's answer on the screen and all you have to do is find the sum that the computer is thinking of.

Your solution needs to have 6 characters and needs the answer to be the one on the top.

Once you have typed your solution then you need to press enter. The characters you have inputted will turn green, orange or gray.

If the character is gray then the character is not in the solution that game is thinking of.
If the character is orange then it is in the solution but not in the right place.
If the character is green then it is in the right place and is in the solution.

You have 6 tries to find the answer that the computer is thinking of and if you find the solution then you win. If you run out of tries then you lose.
